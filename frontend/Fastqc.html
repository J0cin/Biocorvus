QC Inspector Tool

An interactive tool to assess the quality of your FASTQ sequencing data. Upload your files or paste raw text to generate key metrics and visualizations.

How to Use

1. Provide Data: Upload your FASTQ file (.fastq, .fq) or paste the raw text content.

2. Compare (Optional): Check the "Compare two inputs" box to analyze a second file simultaneously.

3. Analyze: Click the "Analyze" button to generate the quality report.

What the Report Shows
  • Summary Statistics: Key metrics like total reads, average length, and GC content.
  • Per Base Quality: A graph showing the quality score distribution across all positions in your reads. Low scores at the beginning or end are common, but a general drop-off indicates a problem.
